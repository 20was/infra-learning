# Learning Principles

## Two kinds of questions
- Action questions: "What command did you run?" → answer with action.
- Reflection questions: "Why do you think that happened?" → answer with observation about yourself or the system.

Both matter. Engineers who only answer action questions never build judgment.

## On reporting
- Complete reports build trust. Partial reports erode it.
- When asked "did you do X?" — list what you did, what you skipped, what got stuck.
- Minimum effort to get to the next step is what AI trained me to do. Infrastructure work punishes it.

## On the journal
- Honest beats polished. Always.
- "I am untrained" not "I am bad." Stories become reality.
